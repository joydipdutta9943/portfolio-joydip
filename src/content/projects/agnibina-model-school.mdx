> Building for the intersection of zero-latency public communication and rigorous, data-heavy compliance reporting.

## The Architecture of Speed

Educational institutions face a unique digital hurdle: they require highly accessible, lightning-fast public portals to broadcast information to parents, while simultaneously needing robust interfaces to handle deep, structurally rigid regulatory data.

The previous monolithic setup was slow, bloated with unused JavaScript, and failed to meet Modern Lighthouse metrics.


## The Execution

I architected a complete frontend overhaul utilizing **Astro v5** and **React 19**.

1. **The Islands Architecture:** By leveraging Astro's partial hydration capabilities (`client:visible` / `client:idle`), we stripped hundreds of kilobytes of unused JavaScript from the main thread.
2. **Design Tokens:** Engineered a bespoke, accessible design system using **Tailwind CSS v4**, guaranteeing responsive geometric layouts across all screen topologies.
3. **Data-Driven Disclosures:** The most critical component was the mandatory _CBSE Disclosure Module_. I built a React pipeline that ingests messy, raw statistical data (enrollments, financial audits, staff metrics) and autonomously formats them into the specific visual tables and structured formats required by government auditors.

### The Impact

- **Ultimate Performance:** Attained pristine **100/100 Lighthouse** scores across Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, and SEO. The aggressive Static Site Generation (SSG) methodology ensured near-instant Time to Interactive (TTI).
- **Automated Compliance:** The standalone React module successfully abstracted away weeks of manual data-entry and formatting, automating the school's entire digital compliance workflow.
