> From investor onboarding to capital calls — automating the entire lifecycle of alternative investment funds.

## The Challenge

Fund managers spend days manually calculating capital calls, generating PDF notices for each investor, and tracking multi-layered capital flows. One miscalculation on a drawdown notice means legal exposure. The process needed full automation with cent-level accuracy.

## Key Engineering Decisions

### Automated Document Pipeline

Headless **Puppeteer** hydrates legal HTML templates with financial data and renders personalized PDF notices per investor. Buffers stream directly to **AWS S3**, bypassing disk I/O entirely — hundreds of investor-specific PDFs generate in parallel during a single capital-call run.

### Relational Financial Ledger

A strict schema via **Drizzle ORM** tracks multi-layered capital flows: `Fund → Scheme → Investor → Commitment → Drawdown`. Every cent is accounted for at each level of the hierarchy.

### Tenant Isolation

A custom `ContextService` middleware automatically scopes every database query to the requesting organization's ID. No query ever runs without a tenant boundary.

## Impact

- **Days → minutes** — Automated PDF generation replaced manual calculation and document assembly
- **Cent-level accuracy** — Relational ledger schema eliminates rounding errors across complex fund hierarchies
- **Complete tenant isolation** — Every query scoped by organization, zero cross-tenant data exposure
