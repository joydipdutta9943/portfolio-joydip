> Connecting farmers and businesses through transparent contract farming — from bid negotiation to day-by-day crop care schedules.

## The Challenge

Contract farming involves real-world constraints: a farmer's land has finite capacity, bids must be validated against physical availability, and once a contract is signed, the farmer needs a concrete daily plan. The platform needed to bridge the gap between digital agreements and agricultural reality.

## Key Engineering Decisions

### Real-Time Negotiation Engine

A transactional bidding system that validates proposed crop quantities against the farmer's actual land availability (`area_in_use`). Resources lock on acceptance, guaranteeing conflict-free contracts.

### Automated Crop Scheduler

Once a contract is signed, a recurring task engine clones a master blueprint into a personalized, day-by-day care schedule (sowing, watering, fertilizing) tailored to the farmer's specific land topology.

### Multi-Persona API

A single Express server exposes three logically separated **GraphQL schemas** — `api/admin`, `api/business_app`, `api/farmer_app` — each with distinct access boundaries, all within one deployable monolith.

## Impact

- **100% conflict-free contracts** — Bids validated against real land capacity before acceptance
- **Automated farmer guidance** — Personalized crop schedules generated instantly on contract signing
- **Clean multi-tenancy** — Three user personas served from one codebase with strict schema separation
